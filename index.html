Data Science

"In Gaia we trust. All others must bring data!" – so goes the adage (or thereabouts).
Though data alone will not an insight make. As a data scientist who has worked in both industry and academia, I know the value of data comes with contextual understanding and domain knowledge, with asking the right questions, and with appropriate tools to extract the information needed. I put this ethos into effect as an analyst within the UK Biobank, in my scientific research, and when meeting the needs of private clients.
